FAQ for students

Is this actually free? What's the catch?

No catch. AppBlocker is open source software under the MIT license. It's 60 lines of Swift code. No account, no email signup, no credit card, no "free tier with limitations." It's free because focus shouldn't cost money.

Will this work on my school Mac?

It works on any Mac running macOS 12 or later. If your school manages your Mac with MDM, you might need admin access to install. Most personal Macs and student-owned Macs work fine.

Can I unblock stuff when I'm done studying?

Yes. Run appblocker unblock "AppName" or appblocker unblock domain.com and it's immediately available again. Block for focus, unblock for downtime.

I have ADHD. Will this help?

AppBlocker can't treat ADHD. But it can remove some of the most tempting distractions from your environment. Many people with ADHD find that removing the option to get distracted is more effective than trying to resist the impulse. It's one tool in the toolbox.

What if I need YouTube for a class?

Unblock it for the specific class session, then re-block it when you're done. The commands take 2 seconds. The key is making distraction the exception, not the default.
